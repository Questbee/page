FAQ

Common questions

Is the Community Edition really free forever?

Yes. The server is MIT licensed and the Android app is a free binary. There are no hidden limits on forms, submissions, or users.

What does "self-hosted" mean?

You run Questbee on your own server — on-premise, a cloud VM, or a private datacenter. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.

Can I try a paid plan before committing?

Yes. Contact us for a 30-day pilot. We support you through setup and your first real data collection.

Do you offer monthly billing?

Yes on Professional and Enterprise. Monthly is 1.5× the annual monthly rate. You can convert to annual any time for a 33% saving.

What counts as a "user"?

A named account that can log in to the web console or submit data via the mobile app. Read-only dashboard viewers count as users too.

Is there non-profit pricing?

Yes. NGOs, academic institutions, and public health agencies qualify for reduced rates. Get in touch with your org details.

Ready to start?

Deploy in minutes on your own server — no credit card required for Community.
